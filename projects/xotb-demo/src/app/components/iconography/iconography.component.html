Iconography

We use symbols and icons to re-authorize our content. Iconography keeps up context between views, gives clearness and direction to actions, and decreases the cognitive burden for users.

xOTB V2 leverages the very popular icon framework Feather Icons. Each icon is designed on a 24x24 grid with an emphasis on simplicity, consistency and usability. With over 200 beautiful and easy to use SVG icons, you are a few stones away to craft clear and communicative UI.
